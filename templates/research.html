No health without mental health...

The article titled "Mental health literacy: A review of the literature" published in The Lancet Psychiatry journal discusses the concept of mental health literacy and presents a review of the literature on this topic. Mental health literacy refers to knowledge and understanding of mental health disorders, including their symptoms, causes, and treatment options, as well as attitudes towards people with mental health problems.
